About

The purpose of the site is to be your go-to spot for the Chinstrap Penguin. The site will allow you to search for Penguins, view magical images of the Chinstrap and also delve into its history and background.


This site is designed to be the one-stop shop for all things chinstrap-penguin. Please use this site to educate yourself and other people around you on the majestic creature that is the chinstrap-penguin


This project was a simulation for a scrum training session on the 26/03/2020 with Duncan Evans
