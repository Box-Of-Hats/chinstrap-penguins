The Duck-billed Platypus: Diet Information

What do I eat?

  • Freshwater Shrimp
  • Annelid Worms
  • Yabby

How much do I eat?

I need to eat about 20% of my own weight each day, which requires me to spend an average of 12 hours daily looking for food.

How often do I eat

I eat daily!
