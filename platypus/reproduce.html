MATING AND REPRODUCTION INFORMATION

The gestation period for the duck billed platypus is at least two weeks (possibly up to a month), and incubation of the eggs takes perhaps another 6 to 10 days.

The females construct specially built nursery burrows, where they usually lay two small leathery eggs.

The young remain protected in the burrow, suckling for three to four months before becoming independent.
