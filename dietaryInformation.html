Diet of the Chinstrap Penguin

What do they eat?

Krill

With an estimated biomass of 379m tonnes of krill in the Southern Ocean, krill makes up for the vast majority of the Chinstrap Penguins diet.

Fish

Squid

Shrimp

How do they hunt?

Chinstrap Penguins feed themselves near the shore, close to their breeding colonies.

Their flippers allow them to pursuit-dive through the water to catch their prey.
